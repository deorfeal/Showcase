What is cryptocurrency arbitrage?

Arbitrage is a trading strategy in which a trader buys and sells the same asset in different markets, profiting from their differences in price.

The mechanics are simple.

If you have $1000 and you buy a coin, which costs $100 on one exchange, then sell it on another exchange, where the same coin is 105$, you will earn 5% = 1050$
