Why Choose OXORIO

People-Oriented Approach

  1. Personalized
    Communication

    Our KYA (Know Your Auditor) approach promotes trust and transparency from the start. Clients have the opportunity to meet their auditors and participate in kickoff calls, ensuring a strong foundation for the audit process.

  2. Client-Specific
    Knowledge Base

    We maintain a knowledge base and audit history for each client, allowing us to provide more contextual and informed assessments in following projects.

Expertise and Team Composition

  1. Experienced Team

    Our auditors bring a minimum of 5 years of experience in the field, ensuring high expertise in every review.

  2. Tailored Audit Teams

    We carefully choose teams of at least three specialists, based on their speciality, ensuring a diverse and comprehensive approach to each audit.

  3. Permanent Team with
    Portfolio Experience

    We have a stable team of auditors that has broad experience with working with unicorn clients, ensuring consistency and reliability.

Audit Process and Scope

  1. Holistic Analysis

    Our audits go beyond the immediate scope, identifying vulnerabilities that may arise from interactions with other modules or systems.

  2. Beyond Smart Contracts

    Our expertise extends to auditing deployment scripts, contract updates, and off-chain infrastructure, providing a holistic security review.

  3. Regular Updates

    Clients receive interim reports every 1-2 weeks, keeping them informed and engaged throughout the audit process.
